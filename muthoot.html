FACTS AND MY VIEWS ABOUT MITS

Campus Life :

Now the classes are conducted online only. But when I visited the campus of Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science it's like heaven. A wonderful campus. It is having a basketball ground. It has a wonderful library with a wide range collection of books. Overall the college is nice but because of the pandemic the classes are now online and it's a very big bad limitation. But these are all part of our life we should face.

Hostel Facilities :

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) offers Hostel facilities with food and accommodation. For students from long distances can have hostels. For a room of four beds for four students is for 5,700 rupees per month including food. So if a student is getting into a hostel from the first year onwards they have to spend 68,400 rupees per year and 2,73,600 rupees for four years. The total fees for both hostel and tuition fee will be about 5,90,000 rupees including caution deposit.
